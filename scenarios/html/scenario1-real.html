When a Y-axis doesn't start at zero, small differences can look massive. Here's the reality:

  • Chevy: 98% still on road
  • Ford: ~97% still on road
  • Toyota: ~96% still on road
  • Nissan: ~95.5% still on road

The actual difference between "best" and "worst" is only about 2.5 percentage points - but the chart makes Chevy look 10x more reliable! Always check where the Y-axis starts.
