Just because two things happen together (correlate) does NOT mean one causes the other. With enough data, you can find correlations between almost anything!

Other spurious correlations include:

  • Divorce rate in Maine correlates with margarine consumption
  • Nicolas Cage films correlate with swimming pool drownings
  • Cheese consumption correlates with deaths by bedsheet tangling

Always ask: Is there a logical reason why A would cause B? If not, it's probably just coincidence!
